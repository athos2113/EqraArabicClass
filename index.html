A 2-Month Arabic Course for English Speaking Students

Enroll


“Learn the Arabic language; it will sharpen your wisdom”

-Umar ibn Khattab (رضي الله عنه)

This course is suitable for all kinds of learners, regardless of ability. Students will be introduced to Arabic grammar in a unique way based on the frequency of vocabulary usage. We will focus completely on the major nominal grammatical constructions.

Online Class

With online lectures, tests, slide Presentations and Ebooks, Students will be engaged in a multimedia-rich environment. The class will be led by Junaid Khan - B.A in Arabic Language and Linguistics (pursuing), B.E (Computer)

FAQs

Where and when do courses take place ?
This course is 100% online. All the lectures are recorded. Students can watch them at their convenience.
What will I learn from this course ?
Students will learn about the Arabic grammatical constructions - parts of speech, nominal/verbal sentences, prepositions, adverbs and pronouns.. They will also learn 200+ new Arabic words. Read "Course Syllabus" for more details
How much does it cost to join the course ?
This course is completely free. Students have complete access to all the lectures, quizzes, assignment and the final exam.
What are the prerequisite for this course ?
Students should know how to read Arabic (with harakat)
Do I need to buy any material ?
We will be using Dr. V. Abdur Rahim's book "Madinah Arabic Reader Book 1". Students will be provided with the soft-copy (ebook). You can either print this copy or buy the book online.
